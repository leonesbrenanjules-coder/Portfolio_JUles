About Jules

Hi! I'm Jules Brenan R. Leones, a college student at Davao del Norte State College studying information technology with a focus on full-stack development and design.

I build things for the web — from backend systems in Laravel and PHP to interfaces designed in Figma and brought to life with HTML, CSS, and React.

My creative process is shaped by anime and film — I find that the best visual storytelling translates directly into better UI decisions and more thoughtful system design.

I'm looking to grow as a developer, contribute to exciting projects, and collaborate with teams who care about both code quality and user experience.

Objective
  • Seeking an opportunity to apply development and design skills in a position that provides room for growth and real-world impact.
  • Enthusiastic about contributing to fast-moving teams where creativity and technical skill intersect.
  • Open to internships, freelance projects, and collaborative builds.
